To get started with AWS. The first thing you need to do is create an AWS account. 

Head over aws.amazon.com and click on the orange "Create an AWS account" button. 

You have to pass in your email address and provide a password with at least 8 characters.


Provide a unique account name. I'm going to use Scrumtuous.

Then provide a little bit of information about yourself.

Agree to the terms of service, and then provide a credit card number.

Then do a verification.

verify your code

And then choose the free, basic support plan.

Then log into the AWS management console and start using Amazon's web services..

And that's all you have to do to get started with AWS!
